How Mappings Work

  • Pattern: A text pattern to match in journal entry descriptions
  • From Account: The account where matching entries are currently posted
  • To Account: The account where entries should be moved to

When you view journals for the "From Account", entries matching the pattern will show suggested changes to move them to the "To Account".
